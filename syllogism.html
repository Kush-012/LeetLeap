1. All roses are flowers. Some flowers fade quickly. Therefore:

A. All roses fade quickly

C. No roses fade quickly

The conclusion follows as 'some' flowers that fade quickly could include roses.

2. No birds are mammals. All bats are mammals. Therefore:
