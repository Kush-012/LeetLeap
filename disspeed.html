1. A car travels 240 km in 4 hours. What is its speed in km/h?

A. 60 km/h
B. 50 km/h
C. 70 km/h
D. 65 km/h
Correct Answer: A. 60 km/h

Speed = Distance ÷ Time
Speed = 240 km ÷ 4 hours = 60 km/h

2. Two trains are moving in opposite directions at speeds of 60 km/h and 40 km/h. What is their relative speed?

A. 20 km/h
B. 100 km/h
C. 80 km/h
D. 50 km/h
Correct Answer: B. 100 km/h

When two objects move in opposite directions, relative speed = Sum of individual speeds
Relative speed = 60 + 40 = 100 km/h

3. A person walks 15 km in 3 hours. How much time will he take to walk 25 km at the same speed?

A. 4 hours
B. 5 hours
C. 6 hours
D. 4.5 hours
Correct Answer: B. 5 hours

Speed = 15 km ÷ 3 hours = 5 km/h
Time = Distance ÷ Speed = 25 km ÷ 5 km/h = 5 hours

4. A train 120 meters long crosses a pole in 8 seconds. What is the speed of the train?

A. 48 km/h
B. 54 km/h
C. 60 km/h
D. 45 km/h
Correct Answer: B. 54 km/h

Speed = Distance ÷ Time = 120 m ÷ 8 s = 15 m/s
Converting to km/h: 15 × 3.6 = 54 km/h

5. Two cars start from the same point and travel in the same direction. Car A travels at 50 km/h and Car B at 70 km/h. After how much time will they be 60 km apart?

A. 2 hours
B. 3 hours
C. 4 hours
D. 2.5 hours
Correct Answer: B. 3 hours

Relative speed = 70 - 50 = 20 km/h
Time = Distance ÷ Relative speed = 60 km ÷ 20 km/h = 3 hours

6. A boat travels 30 km downstream in 2 hours and 18 km upstream in 3 hours. What is the speed of the boat in still water?

A. 12 km/h
B. 10 km/h
C. 15 km/h
D. 9 km/h
Correct Answer: A. 12 km/h

Downstream speed = 30/2 = 15 km/h
Upstream speed = 18/3 = 6 km/h
Speed in still water = (15 + 6)/2 = 12 km/h

7. A man covers half of his journey at 40 km/h and the other half at 60 km/h. What is his average speed for the entire journey?

A. 50 km/h
B. 48 km/h
C. 45 km/h
D. 52 km/h
Correct Answer: B. 48 km/h

For equal distances: Average speed = 2xy/(x+y)
Average speed = (2 × 40 × 60)/(40 + 60) = 4800/100 = 48 km/h

8. Two trains of lengths 100m and 150m are running in opposite directions at speeds of 45 km/h and 55 km/h respectively. In what time will they cross each other?

A. 9 seconds
B. 10 seconds
C. 8 seconds
D. 12 seconds
Correct Answer: A. 9 seconds

Total distance = 100 + 150 = 250m
Relative speed = 45 + 55 = 100 km/h = 100 × 5/18 = 27.78 m/s
Time = 250/27.78 = 9 seconds

9. A cyclist covers a distance of 750m in 2.5 minutes. What is his speed in km/h?

A. 18 km/h
B. 20 km/h
C. 15 km/h
D. 22 km/h
Correct Answer: A. 18 km/h

Speed = 750m ÷ 2.5 min = 750m ÷ 150s = 5 m/s
Converting to km/h: 5 × 3.6 = 18 km/h

10. A and B start from the same point and walk in opposite directions. A walks at 4 km/h and B at 6 km/h. After how much time will they be 25 km apart?

A. 2 hours
B. 2.5 hours
C. 3 hours
D. 2.25 hours
Correct Answer: B. 2.5 hours

Combined speed = 4 + 6 = 10 km/h
Time = Distance ÷ Combined speed = 25 km ÷ 10 km/h = 2.5 hours

11. A car travels from A to B at 60 km/h and returns from B to A at 40 km/h. What is the average speed for the round trip?

A. 48 km/h
B. 50 km/h
C. 45 km/h
D. 52 km/h
Correct Answer: A. 48 km/h

For round trip with equal distances: Average speed = 2xy/(x+y)
Average speed = (2 × 60 × 40)/(60 + 40) = 4800/100 = 48 km/h

12. A train running at 72 km/h crosses a platform 280m long in 25 seconds. What is the length of the train?

A. 180m
B. 220m
C. 200m
D. 250m
Correct Answer: B. 220m

Speed = 72 km/h = 72 × 5/18 = 20 m/s
Total distance = Speed × Time = 20 × 25 = 500m
Train length = 500 - 280 = 220m

13. Two persons A and B start simultaneously from opposite ends of a 200m track. A runs at 8 m/s and B at 12 m/s. When will they meet for the first time?

A. 10 seconds
B. 12 seconds
C. 8 seconds
D. 15 seconds
Correct Answer: A. 10 seconds

Combined speed = 8 + 12 = 20 m/s
Time to meet = Distance ÷ Combined speed = 200m ÷ 20 m/s = 10 seconds

14. A man walks at a speed of 5 km/h to reach the office in time. If he walks at 4 km/h, he reaches 12 minutes late. What is the distance to his office?

A. 4 km
B. 5 km
C. 3 km
D. 6 km
Correct Answer: A. 4 km

Let distance = d km
Time at 5 km/h = d/5 hours
Time at 4 km/h = d/4 hours
d/4 - d/5 = 12/60 = 1/5
d(1/4 - 1/5) = 1/5
d(1/20) = 1/5
d = 4 km

15. A circular track has a circumference of 440m. Two runners start from the same point in the same direction. One runs at 8 m/s and the other at 6 m/s. After how much time will the faster runner lap the slower one?

A. 200 seconds
B. 220 seconds
C. 240 seconds
D. 180 seconds
Correct Answer: B. 220 seconds

Relative speed = 8 - 6 = 2 m/s
Time to complete one lap ahead = Circumference ÷ Relative speed
Time = 440m ÷ 2 m/s = 220 seconds
