1. A bar chart shows monthly sales: Jan-50k, Feb-65k, Mar-40k, Apr-80k, May-70k. What is the percentage increase in sales from March to April?

A. 75%
B. 100%
C. 50%
D. 125%
Correct Answer: B. 100%

Percentage increase = [(80-40)/40] × 100 = (40/40) × 100 = 100%

2. A pie chart shows budget allocation: Education-30%, Healthcare-25%, Defense-20%, Infrastructure-15%, Others-10%. If total budget is ₹500 crores, what amount is allocated to Healthcare and Infrastructure combined?

A. ₹180 crores
B. ₹200 crores
C. ₹175 crores
D. ₹225 crores
Correct Answer: B. ₹200 crores

Healthcare + Infrastructure = 25% + 15% = 40% of ₹500 crores = ₹200 crores

3. A line graph shows temperature variations: Day 1-25°C, Day 2-30°C, Day 3-22°C, Day 4-35°C, Day 5-28°C. What is the average temperature over these 5 days?

A. 26°C
B. 28°C
C. 30°C
D. 27°C
Correct Answer: B. 28°C

Average = (25 + 30 + 22 + 35 + 28) ÷ 5 = 140 ÷ 5 = 28°C

4. A table shows production data: Factory A produces 200 units/day, Factory B produces 150 units/day, Factory C produces 300 units/day. If Factory A works for 20 days, Factory B for 25 days, and Factory C for 18 days, what is the total production?

A. 13,150 units
B. 13,150 units
C. 12,900 units
D. 13,500 units
Correct Answer: B. 13,150 units

Total = (200×20) + (150×25) + (300×18) = 4000 + 3750 + 5400 = 13,150 units

5. A histogram shows frequency distribution of student scores: 0-20 (5 students), 20-40 (12 students), 40-60 (18 students), 60-80 (15 students), 80-100 (10 students). What percentage of students scored above 60?

A. 40%
B. 41.67%
C. 45%
D. 38%
Correct Answer: B. 41.67%

Students above 60 = 15 + 10 = 25. Total students = 60. Percentage = (25/60) × 100 = 41.67%

6. A scatter plot shows correlation between hours studied and marks obtained. If the correlation coefficient is 0.85, what can we conclude?

A. Weak positive correlation
B. Strong positive correlation
C. Strong negative correlation
D. No correlation
Correct Answer: B. Strong positive correlation

Correlation coefficient of 0.85 indicates a strong positive correlation (close to 1.0)

7. A company's quarterly profits are: Q1-₹2.5L, Q2-₹3.2L, Q3-₹2.8L, Q4-₹4.1L. What is the median quarterly profit?

A. ₹2.8L
B. ₹3.0L
C. ₹3.2L
D. ₹3.15L
Correct Answer: B. ₹3.0L

Arranged in order: 2.5, 2.8, 3.2, 4.1. Median = (2.8 + 3.2)/2 = ₹3.0L

A. January
B. February

A. 20%

10. A cumulative frequency curve shows that 60% of students scored below 75 marks and 80% scored below 85 marks. If there are 250 students, how many scored between 75 and 85 marks?

A. 40 students
B. 50 students
C. 60 students
D. 45 students
Correct Answer: B. 50 students

Students between 75-85 = 80% - 60% = 20% of 250 = 50 students

11. A box plot shows data distribution with Q1=15, Q2=25, Q3=35, minimum=5, maximum=50. What is the interquartile range (IQR)?

A. 15
B. 20
C. 25
D. 30
Correct Answer: B. 20

IQR = Q3 - Q1 = 35 - 15 = 20

13. A stacked area chart shows website traffic sources over 6 months. Direct traffic consistently accounts for 40% of total traffic. If total traffic in month 6 was 10,000 visits, how many were from direct traffic?

A. 3,500 visits
B. 4,000 visits
C. 4,500 visits
D. 3,800 visits
Correct Answer: B. 4,000 visits
