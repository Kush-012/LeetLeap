1. What is the next number in the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

A. 42
B. 40
C. 36
D. 45
Correct Answer: A. 42

Pattern: Each number is n(n+1) where n starts from 1. 1×2=2, 2×3=6, 3×4=12, 4×5=20, 5×6=30, 6×7=42

2. Find the missing number: 3, 9, 27, ?, 243

A. 72
B. 81
C. 90
D. 108
Correct Answer: B. 81

Geometric progression with ratio 3: 3¹=3, 3²=9, 3³=27, 3⁴=81, 3⁵=243

3. Complete the series: 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36, ?

D. 64
