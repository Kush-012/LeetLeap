1. If the ratio of two numbers is 3:4 and their sum is 28, what is the smaller number?

A. 10
B. 12
C. 14
D. 16
Correct Answer: B. 12

Let the numbers be 3x and 4x. Their sum is 3x + 4x = 7x = 28. Solving, x = 4. The smaller number is 3x = 3 × 4 = 12.

2. If a:b = 5:7 and b:c = 14:9, what is the ratio a:c?

A. 5:9
B. 7:9
C. 10:9
D. 14:5
Correct Answer: C. 10:9

To find a:c, adjust b in both ratios to be the same. Given a:b = 5:7 and b:c = 14:9, make b equal by multiplying a:b by 2 (since 7 × 2 = 14). So, a:b = 10:14, and b:c = 14:9. Thus, a:c = 10:9.

3. A sum of $120 is divided among A, B, and C in the ratio 2:3:5. How much does C get?

A. $36
B. $48
C. $60
D. $72
Correct Answer: C. $60

The ratio is 2:3:5, so the total parts = 2 + 3 + 5 = 10. C’s share is 5 parts. Amount for C = (5/10) × 120 = $60.

4. If 3 pencils cost $6 and 5 pens cost $15, what is the ratio of the cost of one pencil to one pen?

A. 1:2
B. 2:3
C. 3:2
D. 2:5
Correct Answer: B. 2:3

Cost of one pencil = $6 ÷ 3 = $2. Cost of one pen = $15 ÷ 5 = $3. Ratio of pencil to pen cost = 2:3.

5. If a:b = 4:5 and a is 20, what is the value of b?

A. 20
B. 22
C. 25
D. 30
Correct Answer: C. 25

Given a:b = 4:5, let a = 4x and b = 5x. Since a = 20, 4x = 20, so x = 5. Thus, b = 5x = 5 × 5 = 25.

6. If 4 men can complete a work in 12 days, how many days will 6 men take to complete the same work?

A. 6
B. 7
C. 8
D. 9
Correct Answer: C. 8

The work done is proportional to the number of men and inversely proportional to the number of days. Total work = 4 men × 12 days = 48 man-days. For 6 men, days = 48 ÷ 6 = 8 days.

7. A mixture contains milk and water in the ratio 5:3. If 8 liters of water is added, the ratio becomes 5:4. What is the initial quantity of milk?

A. 20 liters
B. 25 liters
C. 30 liters
D. 40 liters
Correct Answer: C. 30 liters

Let milk = 5x liters and water = 3x liters. After adding 8 liters of water, the ratio is 5x:(3x+8) = 5:4. Solving 5x/(3x+8) = 5/4, we get 20x = 15x + 40, so 5x = 40, x = 8. Milk = 5x = 5 × 8 = 40 liters. 8. If a:b = 2:3 and b:c = 4:5, what is a:b:c?

A. 8:12:15
B. 8:12:15
C. 6:9:12
D. 2:3:5
Correct Answer: B. 8:12:15

10. If 5 kg of rice costs $20, how much will 8 kg of rice cost?

A. $28
B. $30
C. $32
D. $34
Correct Answer: C. $32

Cost is proportional to quantity. Cost of 1 kg = $20 ÷ 5 = $4. Cost of 8 kg = 8 × $4 = $32.

11. The ratio of two quantities is 3:4. If the larger quantity is 24, what is the smaller quantity?

A. 16
B. 18
C. 18
D. 20
Correct Answer: C. 18

Let the quantities be 3x and 4x. The larger is 4x = 24, so x = 6. The smaller quantity = 3x = 3 × 6 = 18.

12. If 3 workers can build a wall in 15 days, how many workers are needed to build it in 9 days?

A. 4
B. 5
C. 6
D. 7
Correct Answer: B. 5

Total work = 3 workers × 15 days = 45 man-days. Workers needed for 9 days = 45 ÷ 9 = 5 workers.

13. A and B invest in a business in the ratio 3:2. If the total profit is $500, how much does A get?

A. $200
B. $250
C. $300
D. $350
Correct Answer: C. $300

The ratio is 3:2, so total parts = 3 + 2 = 5. A’s share = (3/5) × 500 = $300.

14. If a:b = 5:6 and b:c = 3:4, what is a:c?

A. 5:8
B. 5:8
C. 6:5
D. 15:24
Correct Answer: B. 5:8

Make b the same in both ratios. Given a:b = 5:6 and b:c = 3:4, multiply b:c by 2 (3 × 2 = 6). So, b:c = 6:8. Thus, a:c = 5:8.

15. If 4 liters of a solution contains 1 liter of acid, what is the ratio of acid to water?

A. 1:2
B. 1:3
C. 1:3
D. 1:4
Correct Answer: C. 1:3

Total solution = 4 liters, acid = 1 liter, so water = 4 - 1 = 3 liters. Ratio of acid to water = 1:3.
