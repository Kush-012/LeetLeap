1. What is 20% of 150?

A. 20
B. 30
C. 40
D. 50
Correct Answer: B. 30

20% = 0.2, 0.2 × 150 = 30.

2. If a $120 item is discounted by 25%, what is the sale price?

A. $80
B. $85
C. $90
D. $95
Correct Answer: C. $90

Discount = 25% of 120 = 0.25 × 120 = 30. Sale price = 120 − 30 = 90.

3. What percentage is 45 out of 180?

A. 25%
B. 20%
C. 30%
D. 35%
Correct Answer: A. 25%

(45 ÷ 180) × 100 = 25%.

4. If 10% of a number is 20, what is the number?

A. 100
B. 150
C. 200
D. 250
Correct Answer: C. 200

Let the number be x. 0.1x = 20, so x = 20 ÷ 0.1 = 200.

5. A price increases from $40 to $48. What is the percentage increase?

A. 10%
B. 15%
C. 20%
D. 25%
Correct Answer: C. 20%

Increase = 48 − 40 = 8. Percentage increase = (8 ÷ 40) × 100 = 20%.

6. If 30% of a class of 60 students are boys, how many boys are there?

A. 15
B. 18
C. 20
D. 22
Correct Answer: B. 18

30% of 60 = 0.3 × 60 = 18.

7. A number is increased by 20% and then decreased by 20%. What is the net percentage change?

A. 0%
B. 4% decrease
C. 4% increase
D. 2% decrease
Correct Answer: B. 4% decrease

Let the number be 100. Increase by 20%: 100 × 1.2 = 120. Decrease by 20%: 120 × 0.8 = 96. Net change = (96 − 100) ÷ 100 × 100 = −4%.

8. What is 175% of 40?

A. 60
B. 65
C. 70
D. 75
Correct Answer: C. 70

175% = 1.75, 1.75 × 40 = 70.

9. A product’s price is reduced by 20%, and the sale price is $80. What was the original price?

A. $90
B. $100
C. $110
D. $120
Correct Answer: B. $100

Let original price be x. 0.8x = 80, so x = 80 ÷ 0.8 = 100.

10. If 50% of a number is 60, what is 25% of the same number?

A. 25
B. 30
C. 30
D. 40
Correct Answer: C. 30

Let the number be x. 0.5x = 60, so x = 60 ÷ 0.5 = 120. Then, 25% of 120 = 0.25 × 120 = 30.

11. A store offers a 10% discount on a $50 item and then applies a 5% sales tax. What is the final price?

A. $45.00
B. $47.25
C. $47.25
D. $50.00
Correct Answer: C. $47.25

Discount = 10% of 50 = 0.1 × 50 = 5. Price after discount = 50 − 5 = 45. Tax = 5% of 45 = 0.05 × 45 = 2.25. Final price = 45 + 2.25 = 47.25.

12. If 75% of a number is 90, what is 150% of the same number?

A. 120
B. 180
C. 200
D. 210
Correct Answer: B. 180

Let the number be x. 0.75x = 90, so x = 90 ÷ 0.75 = 120. Then, 150% of 120 = 1.5 × 120 = 180.

13. A population of 5,000 increases by 4% annually. What is the population after one year?

A. 5,100
B. 5,200
C. 5,300
D. 5,400
Correct Answer: B. 5,200

Increase = 4% of 5,000 = 0.04 × 5,000 = 200. New population = 5,000 + 200 = 5,200.

14. A number is decreased by 15% to get 85. What is the original number?

A. 90
B. 100
C. 110
D. 120
Correct Answer: B. 100

Let the number be x. 0.85x = 85, so x = 85 ÷ 0.85 = 100.

15. If a price is increased by 25% and then decreased by 20%, what is the net percentage change?

A. 0%
B. 0% change
C. 5% increase
D. 5% decrease
Correct Answer: B. 0% change

Let the number be 100. Increase by 25%: 100 × 1.25 = 125. Decrease by 20%: 125 × 0.8 = 100. Net change = (100 − 100) ÷ 100 × 100 = 0%.
