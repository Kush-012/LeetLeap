1. The area of a rectangle is 120 cm² and its length is 15 cm. What is its perimeter?

A. 30 cm
B. 38 cm
C. 46 cm
D. 52 cm
Correct Answer: C. 46 cm

Width = Area/Length = 120/15 = 8 cm
Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width) = 2 × (15 + 8) = 46 cm

2. The circumference of a circle is 44 cm. What is its area? (Use π = 22/7)

A. 154 cm²
B. 154 cm²
C. 160 cm²
D. 176 cm²
Correct Answer: B. 154 cm²

Radius = Circumference/(2π) = 44/(2 × 22/7) = 7 cm
Area = πr² = (22/7) × 7 × 7 = 154 cm²

6. The total surface area of a hemisphere is 462 cm². What is its diameter? (π = 22/7)
