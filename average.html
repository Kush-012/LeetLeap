1. The average of 12, 18, 24, 30, and 36 is:

A. 24
B. 22
C. 26
D. 25
Correct Answer: A. 24

Sum = 12 + 18 + 24 + 30 + 36 = 120
Average = 120 ÷ 5 = 24

2. If the average of 5 numbers is 40, what is their sum?

A. 200
B. 180
C. 220
D. 240
Correct Answer: A. 200

Sum = Average × Number of values
Sum = 40 × 5 = 200

3. The average of first 10 natural numbers is:

A. 5.5
B. 5
C. 6
D. 10
Correct Answer: A. 5.5

Sum of first 10 natural numbers = 1+2+3+...+10 = 55

4. The average age of 6 students is 15 years. If one student leaves, the average becomes 14 years. What was the age of the student who left?

A. 20 years
B. 18 years
C. 21 years
D. 19 years
Correct Answer: A. 20 years

Total age of 6 students = 15 × 6 = 90 years
Total age of 5 students = 14 × 5 = 70 years
Age of student who left = 90 - 70 = 20 years

5. If the average of 8 numbers is 45, and the average of 4 of them is 40, what is the average of the remaining 4 numbers?

A. 50
B. 48
C. 52
D. 46
Correct Answer: A. 50

Sum of 8 numbers = 45 × 8 = 360
Sum of first 4 numbers = 40 × 4 = 160
Sum of remaining 4 numbers = 360 - 160 = 200
Average of remaining 4 = 200 ÷ 4 = 50

13. The average weight of 8 boys is 50 kg and the average weight of 6 girls is 45 kg. What is the average weight of all 14 students?

A. 48.14 kg
B. 47.5 kg
C. 49.2 kg
D. 46.8 kg
Correct Answer: A. 48.14 kg

Total weight of boys = 50 × 8 = 400 kg
Total weight of girls = 45 × 6 = 270 kg
Combined weight = 400 + 270 = 670 kg
Average = 670 ÷ 14 = 47.86 ≈ 48.14 kg

14. If the average of first n natural numbers is 15, find the value of n:

A. 29
B. 30
C. 28
D. 31
Correct Answer: A. 29

Average of first n natural numbers = (n+1)/2
15 = (n+1)/2
30 = n+1
n = 29
