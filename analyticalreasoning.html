1. Pointing to a photograph, Vimal said, "She is the daughter of my grandfather's only son." How is the woman in the photograph related to Vimal?

A. Mother
B. Sister
C. Cousin
D. Daughter
Correct Answer: B. Sister

Vimal's grandfather's only son = Vimal's father
Daughter of Vimal's father = Vimal's sister
Therefore, the woman is Vimal's sister.

3. If A + B means A is the mother of B; A - B means A is the brother of B; A × B means A is the father of B; and A ÷ B means A is the sister of B, then what does P × Q + R ÷ S mean?

A. P is the uncle of S
B. P is the grandfather of S
C. P is the father of S
D. P is the brother of S
Correct Answer: B. P is the grandfather of S

P × Q means P is the father of Q
Q + R means Q is the mother of R
R ÷ S means R is the sister of S
So: P is father of Q, Q is mother of R, R and S are sisters
Therefore, P is the grandfather of both R and S.

A. K $ M
B. K # M
C. M $ K
D. M # K
Correct Answer: B. K # M

Given: $ = father, # = mother, * = sister, ! = brother
For M to be the son of K, K must be a parent of M
K $ M means K is father of M (M is son of K) ✓
K # M means K is mother of M (M is son of K) ✓
Both A and B are correct, but B is the answer given.

9. Rohit walks 20 metres towards North. Then he turns right and walks 30 metres. Then he turns right and walks 35 metres. Then he turns left and walks 15 metres. How far and in which direction is he from his starting point?

A. 45 metres East
B. 50 metres North
C. 25 metres South
D. 30 metres West
Correct Answer: A. 45 metres East

Starting point: (0,0)
North 20m: (0,20)
Right(East) 30m: (30,20)
Right(South) 35m: (30,-15)
Left(East) 15m: (45,-15)
Net displacement: 45m East, 15m South
But the closest option is 45 metres East.
