1. Using Vedic Mathematics, what is 97 × 103?

A. 9991
B. 9981
C. 10001
D. 10091
Correct Answer: A. 9991

Using (100-3)(100+3) = 100² - 3² = 10000 - 9 = 9991

2. How many factors does 72 have?

A. 10
B. 12
C. 14
D. 16
Correct Answer: B. 12

72 = 2³ × 3². Number of factors = (3+1)(2+1) = 4×3 = 12

3. The average of 5 numbers is 40. If one number 50 is replaced by 80, what is the new average?

A. 46
B. 44
C. 42
D. 48
Correct Answer: A. 46

Increase = 80-50 = 30. New average = 40 + 30/5 = 40 + 6 = 46

4. If 25% of a number is 60, then 40% of the same number is:

A. 90
B. 96
C. 100
D. 84
Correct Answer: B. 96

Number = 60 ÷ 0.25 = 240. 40% of 240 = 0.40 × 240 = 96

5. A shopkeeper marks his goods 40% above cost price and gives a discount of 20%. His profit percentage is:

A. 12%
B. 15%
C. 18%
D. 20%
Correct Answer: A. 12%

Let CP = 100. MP = 140. SP = 140 × 0.8 = 112. Profit% = 12%

6. The difference between compound interest and simple interest on ₹8000 for 2 years at 5% per annum is:

A. ₹20
B. ₹25
C. ₹30
D. ₹40
Correct Answer: A. ₹20

Difference = P(R/100)² = 8000 × (5/100)² = 8000 × 0.0025 = ₹20

7. Find the next term in the series: 5, 11, 23, 47, 95, ?

A. 191
B. 189
C. 193
D. 187
Correct Answer: A. 191

Pattern: ×2 + 1. 5×2+1=11, 11×2+1=23, 23×2+1=47, 47×2+1=95, 95×2+1=191

8. If CODING is written as DPMJOH, then MATHS is written as:

A. NBUFT
B. NBUHT
C. NBUIT
D. NBUUT
Correct Answer: A. NBUFT

Each letter is replaced by the next letter in alphabet: M→N, A→B, T→U, H→I, S→T

9. The ratio of ages of A and B is 3:4. After 6 years, the ratio becomes 4:5. What is A's present age?

A. 18 years
B. 15 years
C. 12 years
D. 21 years
Correct Answer: A. 18 years

Let ages be 3x and 4x. After 6 years: (3x+6)/(4x+6) = 4/5. Solving: x = 6. A's age = 18

10. In what ratio should tea costing ₹60/kg be mixed with tea costing ₹80/kg to get a mixture worth ₹70/kg?

A. 1:1
B. 1:2
C. 2:1
D. 3:2
Correct Answer: A. 1:1

Using alligation: (80-70):(70-60) = 10:10 = 1:1

11. In how many ways can the letters of the word ARRANGE be arranged?

A. 420
B. 630
C. 840
D. 1260
Correct Answer: D. 1260

7 letters with R repeated 2 times, A repeated 2 times: 7!/(2!×2!) = 5040/4 = 1260

12. From a pack of 52 cards, what is the probability of drawing a face card?

A. 3/13
B. 4/13
C. 1/4
D. 1/13
Correct Answer: A. 3/13

Face cards = 12 (J,Q,K of each suit). Probability = 12/52 = 3/13

13. A is the brother of B. B is the sister of C. C is the father of D. How is D related to A?

A. Nephew
B. Niece
C. Son
D. Cannot be determined
Correct Answer: D. Cannot be determined

D could be A's nephew or niece depending on D's gender

14. Rohit walks 10m North, then 15m East, then 10m South, then 5m West. How far is he from the starting point?

A. 8m
B. 10m
C. 12m
D. 15m
Correct Answer: B. 10m

Final position: 10m East of start. Distance = 10m

15. If South-East becomes North and North-East becomes West, then what will South become?

A. North-East
B. North-West
C. South-West
D. East
Correct Answer: A. North-East

The directions are rotated 135° clockwise. South becomes North-East

16. A can do a piece of work in 10 days, B can do it in 15 days. They work together for 4 days, then A leaves. In how many more days will B finish the remaining work?

A. 3 days
B. 4 days
C. 5 days
D. 6 days
Correct Answer: B. 4 days

A's rate = 1/10, B's rate = 1/15. Combined rate = 1/10 + 1/15 = 1/6. Work done in 4 days = 4/6 = 2/3. Remaining work = 1/3. Time for B to finish = (1/3)/(1/15) = 5 days

17. Two pipes A and B can fill a tank in 12 hours and 18 hours respectively. Pipe C can empty the tank in 24 hours. If all three pipes are opened together, in how many hours will the tank be filled?

A. 8 hours
B. 9 hours
C. 10 hours
D. 12 hours
Correct Answer: B. 9 hours

A's rate = 1/12, B's rate = 1/18, C's rate = -1/24. Net rate = 1/12 + 1/18 - 1/24 = 6/72 + 4/72 - 3/72 = 7/72 = 1/9. Time = 9 hours

18. A car travels from A to B at 60 km/hr and returns from B to A at 40 km/hr. What is the average speed for the entire journey?

A. 48 km/hr
B. 50 km/hr
C. 52 km/hr
D. 55 km/hr
Correct Answer: A. 48 km/hr

For equal distances, average speed = 2xy/(x+y) = 2×60×40/(60+40) = 4800/100 = 48 km/hr

19. A train 200m long running at 72 km/hr crosses a bridge in 30 seconds. What is the length of the bridge?

A. 300m
B. 400m
C. 500m
D. 600m
Correct Answer: B. 400m

Speed = 72 km/hr = 20 m/s. Distance covered = 20 × 30 = 600m. Bridge length = 600 - 200 = 400m

20. Statements: All books are pages. Some pages are words. Conclusions: I. Some books are words. II. All words are pages.

A. Only conclusion I follows
B. Only conclusion II follows
C. Both conclusions follow
D. Neither conclusion follows
Correct Answer: D. Neither conclusion follows

From "All books are pages" and "Some pages are words", we cannot conclude that some books are words or all words are pages. The relationship is not definitive.

21. Statements: No cats are dogs. All dogs are animals. Conclusions: I. No cats are animals. II. Some animals are not cats.

A. Only conclusion I follows
B. Only conclusion II follows
C. Both conclusions follow
D. Neither conclusion follows
Correct Answer: B. Only conclusion II follows

Since all dogs are animals and no cats are dogs, some animals (dogs) are definitely not cats. However, we cannot conclude that no cats are animals.

22. The perimeter of a square is 48 cm. What is the area of the square?

A. 120 sq.cm
B. 132 sq.cm
C. 144 sq.cm
D. 156 sq.cm
Correct Answer: C. 144 sq.cm

Side of square = 48/4 = 12 cm. Area = 12² = 144 sq.cm

23. A right triangle has legs of 9 cm and 12 cm. What is the length of the hypotenuse?

A. 13 cm
B. 15 cm
C. 17 cm
D. 21 cm
Correct Answer: B. 15 cm

Using Pythagoras theorem: c² = 9² + 12² = 81 + 144 = 225. c = √225 = 15 cm

24. The angle of elevation of the top of a tower from a point 100m away from its base is 45°. What is the height of the tower?

A. 50 m
B. 75 m
C. 100 m
D. 150 m
Correct Answer: C. 100 m

tan 45° = height/100. Since tan 45° = 1, height = 100 × 1 = 100 m

25. A man is standing on the top of a 40m high building. The angle of depression to a car on the road is 30°. How far is the car from the base of the building?

A. 40 m
B. 60 m
C. 40√3 m
D. 80 m
Correct Answer: C. 40√3 m

tan 30° = 40/distance. Distance = 40/tan 30° = 40/(1/√3) = 40√3 m

26. In a company, 40% employees are engineers, 30% are managers, and the rest are clerks. If there are 60 clerks, what is the total number of employees?

A. 150
B. 200
C. 250
D. 300
Correct Answer: B. 200

Clerks = 100% - 40% - 30% = 30%. If 30% = 60 clerks, then 100% = 60 × 100/30 = 200 employees

27. The bar chart shows sales data for 4 quarters. Q1: 20, Q2: 35, Q3: 45, Q4: 40. What is the percentage increase from Q1 to Q3?

A. 100%
B. 115%
C. 125%
D. 150%
Correct Answer: C. 125%

Percentage increase = ((45-20)/20) × 100 = (25/20) × 100 = 125%

28. A pie chart shows budget allocation: Education 30%, Health 25%, Infrastructure 20%, Others 25%. If the total budget is Rs. 800 crores, how much is allocated to Health and Infrastructure combined?

A. Rs. 300 crores
B. Rs. 320 crores
C. Rs. 340 crores
D. Rs. 360 crores
Correct Answer: D. Rs. 360 crores

Health + Infrastructure = 25% + 20% = 45%. Amount = 45% of 800 = 360 crores

29. The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 3:2. If there are 15 boys, how many girls are there?

A. 8
B. 9
C. 10
D. 12
Correct Answer: C. 10

If boys:girls = 3:2 and boys = 15, then 3x = 15, so x = 5. Girls = 2x = 2 × 5 = 10

30. If West becomes North-East and North becomes South-East, then what will North-West become?

A. South
B. East
C. South-West
D. North-East
Correct Answer: B. East

The directions are rotated 135° clockwise. North-West becomes East after this rotation.
