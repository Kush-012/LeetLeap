1. Two varieties of tea costing ₹30 per kg and ₹50 per kg are mixed in the ratio 3:2. Find the cost price of the mixture per kg:

A. ₹38
B. ₹40
C. ₹42
D. ₹35
Correct Answer: A. ₹38

Cost of mixture = (3×30 + 2×50)/(3+2) = (90+100)/5 = 190/5 = ₹38 per kg

2. In what ratio should milk costing ₹16 per litre be mixed with milk costing ₹20 per litre so that the mixture costs ₹18 per litre?

A. 1:1
B. 2:1
C. 1:2
D. 3:2
Correct Answer: A. 1:1

Using alligation: Ratio = (20-18):(18-16) = 2:2 = 1:1

3. A vessel contains 60 litres of milk. 12 litres of milk is taken out and replaced with water. This process is repeated once more. Find the final quantity of pure milk:

A. 38.4 litres
B. 40 litres
C. 36 litres
D. 42 litres
Correct Answer: A. 38.4 litres

After 1st replacement: 60 × (60-12)/60 = 60 × 48/60 = 48 litres
After 2nd replacement: 48 × (60-12)/60 = 48 × 48/60 = 38.4 litres

4. Two alloys contain gold and silver in the ratios 2:3 and 3:4 respectively. In what ratio should these alloys be mixed to get a new alloy containing gold and silver in the ratio 5:7?

A. 7:10
B. 10:7
C. 3:4
D. 4:3
Correct Answer: A. 7:10

Gold percentage in alloy 1 = 2/5 = 40%
Gold percentage in alloy 2 = 3/7 = 42.86%
Required gold percentage = 5/12 = 41.67%
Using alligation: Ratio = (42.86-41.67):(41.67-40) = 1.19:1.67 ≈ 7:10

5. A mixture of 40 litres contains milk and water in the ratio 3:1. How much water should be added to make the ratio 1:1?

A. 20 litres
B. 15 litres
C. 25 litres
D. 10 litres
Correct Answer: A. 20 litres

Initial milk = 40 × 3/4 = 30 litres
Initial water = 40 × 1/4 = 10 litres
For ratio 1:1, water needed = 30 litres
Water to be added = 30 - 10 = 20 litres

6. The average price of 3 varieties of rice is ₹25 per kg. If two varieties cost ₹20 and ₹22 per kg respectively, find the cost of the third variety:

A. ₹33
B. ₹30
C. ₹35
D. ₹28
Correct Answer: A. ₹33

Total cost of 3 varieties = 25 × 3 = ₹75
Cost of first two = 20 + 22 = ₹42
Cost of third variety = 75 - 42 = ₹33

7. A tank contains 80 litres of pure acid. 20 litres is withdrawn and replaced with water. If this operation is performed 3 times, find the quantity of pure acid left:

A. 33.75 litres
B. 35 litres
C. 30 litres
D. 40 litres
Correct Answer: A. 33.75 litres

Final quantity = 80 × (1 - 20/80)³ = 80 × (3/4)³ = 80 × 27/64 = 33.75 litres

9. A mixture contains alcohol and water in the ratio 4:3. If 14 litres of water is added, the ratio becomes 3:4. Find the original quantity of the mixture:

A. 49 litres
B. 42 litres
C. 56 litres
D. 35 litres
Correct Answer: A. 49 litres

Let original mixture = 7x litres (alcohol = 4x, water = 3x)
After adding water: 4x/(3x+14) = 3/4
16x = 9x + 42
7x = 42
x = 6
Original mixture = 7×6 = 49 litres

B. 2:1

11. From a container containing 50 litres of milk, 10 litres is withdrawn and replaced with water. This process is repeated. Find the ratio of milk to water in the final mixture:

A. 16:9
B. 9:16
C. 4:1
D. 1:4
Correct Answer: A. 16:9

Final milk = 50 × (40/50)² = 50 × 16/25 = 32 litres
Water = 50 - 32 = 18 litres
Ratio = 32:18 = 16:9

13. Three varieties of wheat costing ₹10, ₹15, and ₹20 per kg are mixed in the ratio 2:3:5. Find the cost of the mixture per kg:

A. ₹16.5
B. ₹15
C. ₹17
D. ₹18
Correct Answer: A. ₹16.5

Cost = (2×10 + 3×15 + 5×20)/(2+3+5) = (20+45+100)/10 = 165/10 = ₹16.5 per kg

14. A mixture of 150 litres contains milk and water in the ratio 2:1. How much milk should be added to make the ratio 3:1?

D. 100 litres

Initial milk = 150 × 2/3 = 100 litres
Initial water = 150 × 1/3 = 50 litres
For ratio 3:1, milk needed = 3 × 50 = 150 litres
Milk to be added = 150 - 100 = 50 litres

15. The average salary of all employees in a company is ₹25,000. If the average salary of 7 managers is ₹45,000 and the average salary of remaining employees is ₹20,000, find the total number of employees:

A. 35
B. 40
C. 32
D. 38
Correct Answer: A. 35

Let total employees = n
Total salary = 25000n
Managers' salary = 45000 × 7 = 315000
Others' salary = 20000 × (n-7)
25000n = 315000 + 20000(n-7)
25000n = 315000 + 20000n - 140000
5000n = 175000
n = 35
